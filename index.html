About Us

We are the Science Society for students at Murray Edwards College. Most of our members are NatScis but anyone from any subject with an interest in Science is welcome.

We provide weekly drop-in sessions in the JCR. These allow you to meet students in other year groups, get help and advice, and eat some of the cakes and biscuits provided!

 

We also run parties throughout the year, normally at the start and end of each term. These have previously included Pizza night and Science Themed Cocktails.

 

Careers and academic events are held usually once or twice a term. These may include Fellows Talks events, NatSci Module Options evenings and more.

Essay Competition

Are you an AS Level or GCSE student? Every year we hold an essay competition were we release 4 different titles and then you submit approximatley 2000 words on one of the titles and submit them to us by email or post.
All participants will receive a certificate of participation and there will be prizes for the best essays, as follows:

First Prize: £50
Second Prize: £30
Third Prize: £20
